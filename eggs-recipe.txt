Ingredients:
 - 1.5 Tbsp butter
 - 3 eggs
 - 1 tbsp half-and-half
 - salt and pepper to taste
 
Directions:
 1. Melt the butter over medium heat in a suacepan

 2. Crack the eggs directly into the melted butter.

 3. Stir the eggs and butter with a spatula, breaking up the eggs as you go.

 4. As the eggs start to thicken, take the saucepan off the heat for about 30 seconds.

 5. Alternate on/off the heat for 30 seconds, until the eggs come together.  Remove from the heat.

 6. Add the half-and-half, and stir into the eggs.

 7. Season with salt and pepper and serve.
